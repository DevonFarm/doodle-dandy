About us

Our mission at Doodle Dandy is to enrich your life with the joy of a healthy, happy, and well-socialized Doodle. Every puppy, along with their parents, is raised exclusively in our home, never in a kennel, to foster their smart and loyal nature. We're dedicated to responsibly breeding exceptional companions, and we can't wait to help you welcome your new family member home.

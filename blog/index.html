☀️ Summer Safety for Your Doodle: Essential Heat Protection Tips

June 30, 2025 • Summer Safety & Health
Summer brings joy and outdoor adventures, but it also presents unique challenges for our beloved Doodles. From scorching asphalt to overheating risks, learn the essential safety measures every Doodle owner needs to know to keep their furry family member safe and comfortable during the hot months.

🌡️ Heat Stroke Warning

Watch for: Excess panting, discolored gums, mobility issues. Seek vet care immediately!

🏃‍♂️ Limit Exercise

Watch carefully so they do not over exert in the heat. Early morning or evening walks are best.

🌳 Shade & Water

Always provide protection from the sun and keep them well hydrated with fresh, cool water.

🛣️ Hot Asphalt Test

If it's too hot for your bare feet, it's too hot for their paws! Test with your hand first.

🚗 Never Leave in Cars

Don't leave your Doodle in the car, even with windows cracked. Temperatures can be deadly.

üçΩÔ∏è Complete Meal Preparation

Mix together: Dog food + Coconut flour + Plain yogurt + Pumpkin (or sweet potato) + Warm water

Weekly treats: Old-fashioned oatmeal (not instant, no salt)

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

Kibble, yogurt, pumpkin, coconut flour, and warm water

Step 2: Mix Together

Combine all ingredients with warm water until well mixed

Step 3: Ready to Serve!

Perfect consistency - not too soupy, nicely mixed

‚è∞ CRITICAL First Meal Guidelines

Goal: Baby cleans up everything in 10 minutes or less

First meal: Start with just 1/4 cup kibble + additions

Location: Feed in crate/playpen only

Time limit: 10 minutes maximum - set a timer!

No hovering: Give food, walk away, return after 10 minutes

If not finished: Remove food without fussing - try again next meal

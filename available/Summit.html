⛰️ Summit - The Adventurous Leader

Bold, confident, and naturally commanding! Summit is the natural-born leader of the litter with striking black tri coloring that demands attention. This boy has that special combination of confidence and intelligence that makes him stand out in any crowd.

From day one, Summit has shown exceptional leadership qualities. He's the first to explore new things, the one who leads the pack during play, and shows remarkable problem-solving abilities. But don't mistake his confidence for aggression - this boy is gentle and well-mannered, he just knows what he wants!

Summit would thrive with an active family who appreciates his spirit and can channel his energy into positive activities. He's incredibly smart and will excel at training, outdoor adventures, and any activity that challenges his mind and body. This boy wants to be your adventure partner!

Perfect for families who want:
• Handsome black tri male
• Confident, outgoing personality
• Highly intelligent and trainable
• Natural leader with adventure spirit
• Active companion for outdoor activities
• A dog who's ready for anything

Summit is ready to reach new heights with your family and bring confidence, joy, and adventure to every day. Are you ready for this remarkable boy?

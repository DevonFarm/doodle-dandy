🌟 About Flurry

🌬️ Flurry - The Dancing Snowflake

Light, graceful, and absolutely delightful! Flurry is the petite black and white girl who moves through life with the grace of her namesake. Small for the litter, she makes up for it with boundless personality and the sweetest, most joyful disposition you could imagine.

This little lady is pure happiness on four legs. She has that special lightness about her - both in her movements and her spirit. Flurry is playful without being overwhelming, affectionate without being needy, and has a gentle nature that makes her perfect for families with children or anyone looking for a sweet companion.

Despite being one of the smaller pups, Flurry has confidence and spunk! She holds her own during play but is equally happy being the gentle sweetheart. Her beautiful black and white markings are striking, and her personality is just as captivating. This girl brings joy wherever she goes!

Perfect for families who want:
• Stunning black & white coloring
• Smaller, graceful build
• Sweet, gentle personality
• Great with children
• Playful yet not overwhelming
• Pure joy and love in one package

Flurry is ready to dance into your life and bring lightness, love, and pure happiness to every day. Are you ready for this dancing snowflake?

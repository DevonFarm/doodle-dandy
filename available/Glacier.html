🏔️ Glacier - The Majestic One

Stunning, powerful, and absolutely magnificent! Glacier is the show-stopping black and white male who commands attention simply by being present. His striking coloring and impressive build make him look like he stepped out of a painting, but his regal appearance is matched by an equally impressive temperament.

This boy moves with a grace and confidence that's captivating to watch. He's calm and composed, with that mature demeanor that makes him seem wise beyond his weeks. Glacier isn't in a rush - he observes, considers, and then acts with purpose. It's a quality that makes him incredibly easy to be around.

Despite his majestic presence, Glacier is incredibly gentle and affectionate with his people. He bonds deeply and shows his love through quiet companionship. He'd be perfect for a family who appreciates his noble nature and can provide the structure and consistency that helps him thrive.

Perfect for families who want:
• Stunning black & white markings
• Large, impressive stature
• Calm, mature temperament
• Regal yet gentle presence
• Deep loyalty and affection
• A truly magnificent companion

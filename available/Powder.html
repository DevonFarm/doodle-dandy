🧸 Powder - The Fluffy Sweetheart

Soft, cuddly, and impossibly sweet! Powder is the Blue Merle Tri teddy bear of the litter with the fluffiest coat you'll ever touch. His stunning markings and incredibly soft fur make him look like a premium stuffed animal come to life - but he's even better because he's real!

This boy is pure sweetness wrapped in fur. He loves cuddles more than anything and has mastered the art of the puppy snuggle. Powder is that special pup who seems to know when you need comfort, always ready with gentle affection and his soft, warm presence.

Despite being a total love bug, Powder also has a playful side. He enjoys romps with his siblings and is always up for a good game, but his favorite activity is definitely snuggling with his people. His gentle, affectionate nature makes him perfect for families who want a devoted, loving companion.

💎 Icey - The Cool Companion

Calm, collected, and oh-so-cool! Icey is the tri-color beauty with the perfect chill personality. Her stunning markings - rich black, warm rust, and crisp white - create an absolutely gorgeous combination that turns heads everywhere she goes.

This girl has mastered the art of being perfectly balanced. She's playful when it's time to play, calm when it's time to relax, and always seems to know exactly what's needed in any situation. Icey has that special "old soul" quality that makes her incredibly easy to be around.

With her composed demeanor and gentle nature, Icey would be perfect for families looking for a dog who can adapt to any lifestyle. Whether you're active adventurers or cozy homebodies, she'll be happy as long as she's with her people. Her calm confidence makes her great with children and other pets.

Perfect for families who want:

• Calm, balanced temperament
• Adaptable to any lifestyle
• Great with children and pets
• Easy-going yet playful
• The perfect chill companion

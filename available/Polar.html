🌟 About Polar

🐻 Polar - The Gentle Giant

Big, beautiful, and incredibly gentle! Polar is the largest male in the litter, with a striking black and white coat that's absolutely stunning. Despite his size, this boy is the definition of a gentle giant - calm, patient, and incredibly sweet-natured.

Polar has that wonderful laid-back temperament that makes him perfect for families. He's playful when it's time to play but equally content to just be near his people, quietly observing the world. His calm confidence is evident in everything he does - never pushy or demanding, just a steady, reliable presence.

This boy is exceptional with children and shows remarkable patience. He's the type of dog who will sit calmly while kids climb over him, then gently play when they want action. His size might be impressive, but his gentle spirit is what truly makes him special.

Perfect for families who want:
• Large, impressive black & white male
• Incredibly gentle temperament
• Patient with children
• Calm, steady personality
• Loyal companion who's never pushy
• The perfect gentle giant

Polar is ready to bring his big heart and gentle soul to your family. Are you ready for this magnificent boy?

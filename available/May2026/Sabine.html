🎨 About Sabine

Mystery looks good on her.

Meet Sabine — black tri girl, solid black face, collar sitting right, and an air about her that makes you want to know more. Born May 4th, and already the most intriguing one in the room without doing a single thing to earn that title.

That solid black face is striking. Clean, bold, no distractions — just presence. While others wear their markings loudly, Sabine keeps hers close. Controlled. Intentional. She'll show you what she wants you to see and not a thing more, until she decides she trusts you.

And when she does, watch out.

Behind that mysterious exterior is a dog with depth — creative, perceptive, fiercely loyal to her people once she's claimed them. Sabine doesn't do surface level. She doesn't do casual. When she loves you, you'll know it, and you'll wonder how you ever felt loved before.

For the family that appreciates a dog with layers. She has them. All of them.

👑 About Leia

Royalty doesn't announce itself. It simply is.

Meet Leia — merle girl, with a spot sitting right in the center of her white stripe like it was placed there on purpose. Because maybe it was. Born May 4th, into a litter that somehow produced a princess, and none of us are surprised.

That centered spot is everything. Precise. Intentional. Perfectly positioned — like a crown worn exactly right. Leia's merle coat does what merle does best, shifting and swirling, never quite the same twice, always beautiful. And right in the middle of all of it, that mark. Unmistakable. Hers alone.

She carries herself like she knows. Not arrogance — grace. There's a difference, and Leia is the difference.

She'll be devoted to her family in that deep, unwavering way that makes you feel chosen. Because with Leia, you are. She doesn't give that loyalty casually. She gives it completely, to the right people, and then she never takes it back.

A princess and a best friend, all in one. The Force really outdid itself on this one.

� About Rose

The details are where the magic is.

Meet Rose — tri girl, collar right, with a tiny spot on the back of her neck and the most delicate little paint dip on her tail. Born May 4th, and proof that you don't have to be the loudest one in the room to be the most memorable.

Look closely and you'll find her. That's always been the way with Rose. A tiny spot here. A whisper of color there. Markings that reward the people who slow down, pay attention, and appreciate the small things — because Rose is a small thing, in the very best sense.

She's going to be steady. Warm. The dog that ends up in the middle of every family photo not because she pushed to the front but because no one could imagine the picture without her. Quietly essential. Consistently there.

Some dogs announce themselves. Rose just becomes part of you, gradually and completely, until one day you realize you can't remember what it was like before her.

🌀 About Han Solo

He didn't choose the name. The name chose him.

Born May 4th — because of course he was — Han Solo is our blue merle boy, and from the moment he arrived he's been doing exactly what he wants, exactly when he wants. No apologies. No explanations. Just Han.

That merle coat tells its own story. Swirled, unpredictable, one of a kind — no two patches the same, no blueprint, no map. You don't follow Han Solo. You just try to keep up.

He's going to be the dog that makes strangers stop their cars. The one who walks into a room and rearranges it without trying. Confident without being loud about it. Charming without working for it.

Somewhere out there is the family that's been waiting for exactly this — the dog who keeps life interesting, who meets every adventure like he already knew it was coming.

Han Solo doesn't do ordinary. Neither does his family.

May the Force be with you, Han Solo. 💙

� About Baby Yoda

Small. Wise. Impossible to ignore.

Meet Baby Yoda — our flashy black tri boy with tall white socks that go all the way up, and a collar sitting just right. Born May 4th into a litter of twelve, and somehow he's already the one everyone notices first.

There's something about him. You can't explain it. You just feel it.

Those white socks weren't an accident. The Force dresses its favorites a little differently — a little louder, a little more look at me — and Baby Yoda showed up ready. Flashy from birth. Unbothered always.

He's going to be the dog that sits in your lap like he owns it. The one that figures out every routine before you've finished establishing it. Tuned in. Turned up. Completely calm about all of it.

Patient. Perceptive. Quietly running the whole operation.

Whoever gets this boy isn't just getting a dog. They're getting a presence. A companion. The kind of soul that makes a house feel different — warmer, fuller, like something that was missing finally arrived.

And it did. On May 4th. Obviously.

🌟 About Wren

She arrived loud and she has no intention of quieting down.

Meet Wren — tri girl, four whites, paint dip markings, and a full collar that suits her perfectly because Wren does nothing halfway. Born May 4th, and from the very first moment she made sure everyone in that whelping box knew she was here.

Four whites. Paint dip. Full collar. That's not a puppy that blends in — that's a puppy that was designed to be seen. Bright, bold, beautifully marked, like someone turned up every setting just slightly past the rest.

She's going to be the dog with opinions. The one who has a spot on the couch that is her spot, a schedule that is her schedule, a family that is her family — and she will love them with everything she has, completely and without reservation, as long as everyone understands how this works.

Wren isn't for the faint of heart. She's for the family that wanted a little more life in the room and means it.

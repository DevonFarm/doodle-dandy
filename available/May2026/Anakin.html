🔥 About Anakin

He came in with a full collar and something to prove.

Meet Anakin — black tri boy, marked from the start, born on May 4th into a litter that didn't know what hit it. Twelve puppies and somehow this one already takes up more space than his size suggests. Not in a bad way. In a this dog has a destiny kind of way.

Full collar means bold markings. Clear edges. No ambiguity about who he is or where he stands. Anakin doesn't blend. Anakin doesn't fade into the background. He shows up fully, completely, all the way — and he expects the same from the people around him.

He's going to be brilliant. Probably too smart for his own good, at least for a while. The kind of dog that needs a job, a purpose, something worthy of what he's capable of. Give him that and you'll never stop being amazed.

The right family for Anakin isn't just ready for a dog. They're ready for a partner.

☁️ Skye - The Dreamy Explorer

This beautiful black tri girl has her head in the clouds and her heart full of adventure. With her stunning classic Bernedoodle markings and gentle demeanor, Skye embodies everything wonderful about the breed. Her soft, fluffy coat and sweet expression will melt your heart instantly!

Skye is the puppy who approaches life with curiosity and wonder. She's gentle yet adventurous, calm yet playful, and has that special intuitive nature that makes Bernedoodles such incredible companions. She loves exploring new places and experiences, but always stays close to her people.

This sweet girl has shown exceptional socialization skills from day one. She's confident without being pushy, affectionate without being clingy, and her gentle nature makes her perfect for families of all types. Skye would thrive with a family who loves outdoor adventures, cozy cuddle sessions, and everything in between.

Perfect for families who want:
• Classic black tri Bernedoodle beauty
• Gentle, intuitive companion
• Great with children and other pets
• Loves adventure but equally happy at home
• Calm confidence with sweet affection
• A loyal friend for life
